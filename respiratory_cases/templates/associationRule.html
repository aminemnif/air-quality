Association Rules Analysis

Key Findings

Using the Apriori algorithm, we identified significant relationships between pollutants and sectors, which provide critical insights into emission patterns:

  • VOC and PM25-PRI frequently co-occur with PM10-PRI (confidence: 100%).
  • Organic Carbon and PM25-PRI are strong precursors to PM10 (lift: 1.88).
  • PM25-PRI, PM10-PRI, NOX, and VOC reliably lead to increased CO levels.

Data Preparation

The EPA dataset was prepared by categorizing emissions into "High" and "Low" based on median thresholds and formatting data for frequent itemset generation.

Visualization

Network graphs and heatmaps highlighted pollutant relationships, with PM10 and VOC playing central roles in emissions.

Conclusion

These insights help policymakers target key pollutants, especially PM10 and VOC, to improve air quality.
